A Little About Me

I'm Ting, a consultant currently based in Nairobi, Kenya, working on social impact and international development projects.

My journey has taken me across the globe, working and living in Shanghai, Beijing, Mexico City, London, and New York before landing in Nairobi. My background is at the intersection of computer science and business, and I've spent the last few years working on things like agentic AI, autonomous vehicles, and urban development. I'm interested in how these technical systems can be built to be more useful and equitable in the real world.

Outside of work, I spend my time backpacking, scuba diving, running, and reading — mostly memoirs, contemporary fiction, and sci-fi.
